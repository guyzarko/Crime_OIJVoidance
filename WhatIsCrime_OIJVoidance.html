Crime_OIJVoidance

Description --The crime rates in cities seems to be increasing. It is believed that crime is not increasing nor decreasing but social media has made it easier to report and that is why it seems to be increasing. Our group aims to take the City of Chicago’s crime history and analyze it to see which neighborhoods are in fact safe or not.

Technologies --GitHub Repository, TensorFlow, Command Prompt, Python

Marketability --The crime rates in cities seems to be increasing. Our group aims to take the City of Chicago’s crime history and analyze it to see which neighborhoods are in fact safe or not. This is a marketable project due to the high demand of understanding crime and seeing if it is increasing or decreasing.
